Dr. Maria [Masha] Okounkova

Flatiron Research Fellow in computational astrophysics

I go by Masha, which is a Russian diminutive of Maria.
My pronouns are she/her/hers.

I am a Flatiron Research Fellow at the Center for Computational Astrophysics at the Flatiron Institute in New York City. I got my PhD in physics from Caltech in 2019 and my AB in physics from Princeton in 2014. My research is in the field of numerical relativity, and I am a member of the SXS collaboration and the LIGO scientific collaboration
